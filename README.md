# AIPMBOK
A Body of Knowledge for AIPMs

The Body of Knowledge alone is insufficient.
We need a structured curriculum to understand the Topics, Frameworks, Research, Patterns, underlying Platformss and Tools. 
So I've created a Track-wise learning, spread across 15-20 content (text & video) for each track. 
As we progress across the weeks, we will update the AIPMBOK and also the content depth. 
Based on feedback, conversations and reviews, I'll add a ton of more content to this. 

I hope this provides value and helps you understand the concepts at a depth and simplicity that no other content on the internet provides.
